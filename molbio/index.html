Genetics and Molecular Biology

Our lab studies topics in clinical and molecular genetics. We have concentrated on research projects involving Marfan syndrome and related disorders of the aorta, the molecular biology of fracture healing, and more recently the molecular genetics of ataxia and GPI anchor deficiencies. Much of our research has a strong computational component guiding the “wetlab” experiments.
